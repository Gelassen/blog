--- layout: post title: 'The root cause of a conflict' date: '2017-10-07' author: Gelassen tags: modified_time: '2017-10-07' --- The root cause of the conflict is scarcity. We move into conflict situation when there is not enough something for everyone - material resources, opinions about the Truth, decision regarding what to do next etc.

Therefore if we want gracefully resolve the conflict we have to generate more value or/and decrease our expectations.

The issue is there is still a limit on how much we can decrease our expectations and how much value we are able to generate.
