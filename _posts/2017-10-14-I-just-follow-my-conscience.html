--- layout: post title: 'I just follow my conscience' date: '2017-10-14' author: Gelassen tags: modified_time: '2017-10-14' --- Some people call me as a chess player or person without a plan on life, but what I do is just follow my conscience and serve aims greater than I am. However the one who gave me conscience is indeed a superior to all masters in chess.

I do mistakes, big and small, but they are inevitable on path of learning, doing something new to change things to the better.

For a while I was driven with idea to build a society according to my vision how things should work. I believed we can unite all people under same values, I believed we can do this by patience to mistakes and creating opportunities for thinking, reflection and enlightenment. This world become much more complex and diverse, life proved me I can't rely only on my own views and views of people from my sandbox. I have to be more humble, seek for contribution of wider groups of people and listen more.

It moved my focus, I found my new mission in professional and personal development. Markets are responsible for civilization and everything I learned for the passed year shown me all my efforts in six years in IT were applied in the wrong direction (*), but may be that's what we call experience, isn't it?
