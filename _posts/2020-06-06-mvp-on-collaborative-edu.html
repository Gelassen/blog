Released a MVP that implement the idea of collaborative education.


Released a MVP that implement the idea of collaborative education. Nowadays there is exponential growth of information around us and constant pressure to learn new things. How to filter what is a valuable and what is not? Recommendations. Let's people with the same needs and goals filtrate what works and does not. The same as google search engine counts references on the content before show it to you on search result page, we will count votes for educational course. In user stories MVP looks as: As an Author I create a set of courses to achieve a goal. As a Member of community I offer an alternative data source of a course. As a Member of community I increase the rate of the data source. Next steps are involve the community to create & use courses, gather feedback and adopt the product to community needs. [origin target audience are russian speakers, however it is not limited to single language] https://lnkd.in/d7XNGAR https://lnkd.in/dkVzYBu Tech stack: Android, Yandex cloud, Kotlin, NodeJS, MySQL, Sentry.io
