--- layout: post title: 'High performance team needs a psychological safety' date: '2017-10-21' author: Gelassen tags: modified_time: '2017-10-21' --- High performance team needs a psychological safety and I have to admit it is true.

On a biological level we have several layers of brain which scientists explain evolved with time. The most recent one is neocortex which is responsible for our major thinking processes. Under severe stress ancient level of brain responsible for instincts start to dominate and suppress neocortex with our rational thinking processes. For me, as a software engineer, it means drop in my ability to solve problems and write code.

Since I left corporate world level of uncertainty grown a lot and forced me to quickly learn new techniques how to stay productive in all our activities under stress https://goo.gl/rAbdvK

I mentioned here https://lnkd.in/gqYmEZT to run a company/business the first thing we should track is available cash with some buffer on future. Coderbunker offers our clients retainer contracts where our clients transfer budget at the beginning of a month and get back unused money at any time when they want to stop work. My personal experience working under different scheme shown delay with payments or mess in processes take attention from major work.

There is still a space for experiments.
