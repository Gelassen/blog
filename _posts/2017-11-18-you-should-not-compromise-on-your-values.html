--- layout: post title: 'You should not compromise on your values' date: '2017-11-18' author: Gelassen tags: modified_time: '2017-11-18' --- Many years ago from Adizes lectures I learned about company lifecycle and challenges company faced with on each stage of its growth.

It always reminds me about two important things.

Everything which is alive has problems, we can not really avoid them -- only choose which one we will deal with. If so it would be good to do not compromise on our values and beliefs and make actions according to them.

The only constant in our lifes is change. Change in us or outside environment and we have to learn how to embrace it and be comfortable with it.

Be open for change and do not compromise on your values&beliefs.
