--- layout: post title: 'About social systems of coordinates' date: '2017-08-12' author: Gelassen tags: modified_time: '2017-08-12' --- When I was a bit younger I had been preparing to university exams and intensely study physics.

When I faced with complex task I was surprised that the task seems to don't have a solution can be easily solved when I changed system of coordinates. When system of coordinates is changed the issue is disappeared.

It is an interesting observation -- the presence or absence of the problem depends on the system of coordinates. For people this system of coordinates is their world view, their ideals and their values (their culture).

We should keep it in mind when we communicate to another person our problem. It is quite possible in his system of coordinates this problem doesn't exist.

The most important talk is a talk about definitions, values and ideals.
