--- layout: post title: 'When I stopped attempts to find balanced solution and unite all people under same values date: '2017-04-18' author: Gelassen tags: modified_time: '2017-04-18' --- Dear friends and colleagues,

Two years ago I made a courage step in unknown to follow what I believe in. It was a beginning of amazing journey: I saw a lot, I did many new things and made many mistakes. Finally I learned from them and understand many new things I didn't aware of early.
For those I inspired -- I don't believe anymore in universal truth and vision. This world became much more complex and diverse; disagreement and conflict followed by change leads to a new stable state and this is inevitable part of movement forward. Now I keep what is important for some group of people and me just for ourselves and make a step back to let others shine with their own ideas and visions.
For those I brought issues -- thank you for your support and patience to my growth, thank you for supportive environment where I can do something, make mistakes and learn from this.

It is something marvelous to share this today when we-Christians celebrate Easter as in some way it is the death of me old and the birth of me new. Happy Easter!
